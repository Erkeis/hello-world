Who should have the 'Final Word' on Objective Achievement?

Designing the verification layer for the Agent Farm

A

Automated "Quality Gates"

Orchestrator runs automated tests/linters. If they fail, the Subagent is told to "Try again" without ever bothering you.

  • ✅ Maximum scale; you only see finished work
  • ❌ Hard to automate "Taste" or subjective goals
B

CEO Approval (Manual)

Orchestrator gathers all subagent artifacts and presents a "Consolidated Report". Nothing is finalized until you click [Approve].

  • ✅ Perfect control over quality and "Taste"
  • ❌ You remain a partial bottleneck for flow
C

Hierarchical Delegation

A "Lead Agent" (Sub-PM) reviews the work of others. Only major milestones or unsolvable conflicts are escalated to the User.

  • ✅ Best of both worlds; mimics a real team
  • ❌ Most complex to implement and debug
